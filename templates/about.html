About FibroTracker

FibroTracker is a comprehensive health monitoring application designed specifically for individuals living with Fibromyalgia. Our mission is to empower users to understand their symptoms, identify triggers, and communicate more effectively with healthcare providers.

Key Features

  • Daily symptom and activity logging
  • Interactive pain mapping
  • Sleep and weather sensitivity tracking
  • Weekly and monthly analytics
  • AI-powered insights and chatbot support

Our Team

We are a dedicated team of developers and health enthusiasts aimed at making chronic illness management easier and more accessible.
